Reporting Guidelines
Before Submitting a Report:
  • Be as detailed and accurate as possible
  • Include dates, times, and locations when available
  • Describe any visible injuries or emotional states
  • Do not confront the suspected abuser
What Happens Next:
  1. Your report will be reviewed by an administrator
  2. A case ID will be assigned
  3. The case will be assigned to appropriate authorities
  4. You will receive updates through your dashboard
If a child is in immediate danger, please call emergency services immediately!
Report Privacy

Your personal information is kept confidential and is only shared with authorized personnel involved in the case.

You can track the status of your report using the case ID that will be assigned after submission.

Evidence Guidelines
Acceptable Evidence:
  • Photos of injuries or conditions
  • Videos showing abuse or neglect
  • Audio recordings of relevant conversations
  • Medical reports or school records
  • Witness statements
Important Notes:
  • All evidence is confidential and encrypted
  • Only authorized authorities can access your uploads
  • Do not endanger yourself when collecting evidence
  • Do not alter or edit any evidence files
Evidence submitted may be used in legal proceedings if necessary.
