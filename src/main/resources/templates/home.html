Who We Are

About CARS

Dedicated to creating safer environments for all children through protection, intervention, and advocacy

Our mission is to protect every child from abuse and neglect.

CARS was established to provide a comprehensive system for reporting, tracking, and responding to child abuse cases while connecting children and families with vital support services.

  • Create safe, accessible channels for reporting suspected child abuse.
  • Ensure rapid response and intervention in cases of child endangerment.
  • Connect children and families with appropriate support services and resources.

Our team consists of child protection specialists, social workers, counselors, and technology experts working together to safeguard children's wellbeing. We collaborate closely with law enforcement, schools, healthcare providers, and community organizations to create a comprehensive safety net for vulnerable children.

Knowledge is the first step in protecting children.

We provide comprehensive resources for parents, educators, healthcare professionals, and community members to recognize the signs of abuse, understand reporting obligations, and help prevent child maltreatment.

  • Guidelines for mandatory reporters and concerned citizens
